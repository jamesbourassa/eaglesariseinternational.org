About the founder and mission

This ministry flows from decades of prayer, Scripture study, Christian service, and a long-standing burden for evangelism, deliverance, healing, discipleship, and the truth of the Rapture and the End Times.

A life shaped by prayer, service, teaching, evangelism, and faith in Jesus Christ.
Early years of calling, compassion, and ministry.

I have studied the Living Word of God for over 60 years. There are many mysteries hidden within Scripture that are released over time to those who search and ask. I have received End Time visions and dreams since I was seven years old, and over the years they have inspired me to keep the lamp burning in myself and in others.

By the age of 12, I had already begun ministering to girls at school regarding the sin of abortion. I often brought pregnant girls home with me, and my mother and I would talk with their parents to help restore them to their homes. Some gave their babies up for adoption, and some kept them. I still support pro-life movements today.

I married and had five beautiful children, each with their own unique gifting, talents, and personalities. During the birth of our second son, I died and was brought back by the prayers of my mother and husband. From that point forward, I understood in a deeper way that every day is precious and chose to live fully in service to the Lord God Almighty.

Over the years, I served as a Sunday school teacher, youth leader, associate pastor, counselor, deliverer, teacher, preacher, prophet, and apostle. The trials were many, but so were the blessings and the Lord’s protection. I learned that authority is tied to coming under God-given authority.

I went through a born-again experience at age 26 and received the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the edification of speaking in tongues. I was called into deliverance ministry, a work that often faces spiritual opposition. In that calling, we traveled through eastern and midwestern states and saw many people set free and healed.

Evangelism has always remained central, because salvation is even more important than healing. I have mentored and taught many for the Kingdom of God and His people, and I continue to carry a strong calling to teach truth and point people toward Jesus Christ.

Mission emphasis: prayer, evangelism, discipleship, deliverance, teaching, revival, and reaching people across the nations through both personal ministry and digital outreach.
