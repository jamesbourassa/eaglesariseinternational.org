Our heart is to proclaim Christ from Jerusalem to the ends of the earth, while praying intentionally for seven of the world’s largest metropolitan areas.

A ministry for the nations

Ours is a global ministry with a mission of reaching the lost and equipping the saints in seven of the world’s largest metropolitan areas and beyond. Our vision is to help bring the Kingdom of Heaven to Earth and to be part of a great harvest of souls in this generation.

Global reach through digital evangelism

The internet makes it possible to reach millions of people effectively across cultures and demographics without geographical boundaries. We use online platforms to meet people in their moment of need with truth, hope, and the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Why this matters now

Millions of people around the world are asking deep questions: Do I matter? Is there a God? What is my purpose in life? We believe the answer is found in Jesus Christ, and that this moment in history gives the Church an unprecedented opportunity to reach people before it is too late.

Our mission in practice

We are expanding our mission field to reach people we may never have the chance of touching in person. They need to know that Jesus Christ is the only One who gives true purpose and fulfills the deepest need of the human heart.

Truth for seekers

Many are searching for answers about God, purpose, eternity, and meaning. We seek to offer Christ-centered teaching with compassion and clarity.

Equipping the saints

We want believers to remain watchful, grounded in Scripture, and strengthened in prayer, worship, holiness, and endurance.

Global prayer and outreach

Our heart extends across continents and cultures. We support prayer, evangelism, and encouragement for people from every background, with a special burden for the nations.

Join us in prayer, outreach, and witness

With dark forces arrayed against truth, this is no time for silence. Christians need courage, clarity, and freedom to express Biblical convictions without fear.
