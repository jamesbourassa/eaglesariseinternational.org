A ministry for the nations

Ours is a global ministry with a mission of reaching the lost and equipping the saints in seven of the world’s largest metropolitan areas and beyond. Our vision is to help bring the Kingdom of Heaven to Earth and to be part of a great harvest of souls in this generation.

Global reach through digital evangelism

The internet has made it possible to reach millions of people effectively across cultures and demographics, without geographical boundaries. We use online platforms to meet people in their moment of need with truth, hope, and the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Why this matters now

Millions of people around the world are asking deep questions: Do I matter? Is there a God? What is my purpose in life? We believe the answer is found in Jesus Christ, and that this moment in history gives the Church an unprecedented opportunity to reach people before it is too late.

About the founder and mission

This ministry flows from decades of prayer, Scripture study, Christian service, and a long-standing burden for evangelism, deliverance, healing, discipleship, and the truth of the Rapture and the End Times.

A life shaped by prayer, service, teaching, evangelism, and faith in the Lord Jesus Christ.
Early years of calling, compassion, and ministry.

I have studied the Living Word of God for over 60 years. There are many mysteries hidden within Scripture that are released over time to those who search and ask. I have received End Time visions and dreams since I was seven years old, and over the years they have inspired me to keep the lamp burning in myself and in others.

By the age of 12, I had already begun ministering to girls at school regarding the sin of abortion. I often brought pregnant girls home with me, and my mother and I would talk with their parents to help restore them to their homes. Some gave their babies up for adoption, and some kept them. I still support pro-life movements today.

I married and had five beautiful children, each with their own unique gifting, talents, and personalities. During the birth of our second son, I died and was brought back by the prayers of my mother and husband. From that point forward, I understood in a deeper way that every day is precious and chose to live fully in service to the Lord God Almighty.

Over the years, I served as a Sunday school teacher, youth leader, associate pastor, counselor, deliverer, teacher, preacher, prophet, and apostle. The trials were many, but so were the blessings and the Lord’s protection. I learned that authority is tied to coming under God-given authority. Like others, I have fallen at times, but the Lord has forgiven me, lifted me up, and kept me moving forward.

I went through a born-again experience at age 26 and received the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the edification of speaking in tongues. I was called into deliverance ministry, a work that often faces spiritual opposition. In that calling, we traveled through eastern and midwestern states and saw many people set free and healed. Yet evangelism has always remained central, because salvation is even more important than healing.

I was trained under wonderful pastors who taught and mentored me with patience and love. Some are widely known and some are not, but all were precious to me. In turn, I have mentored and taught many for the Kingdom of God and His people.

God has done many miracles in my life, in my family, and in the lives of many others. Being a leader means being a servant, as Jesus was. My legacy is not material but spiritual. It is the fulfillment of many dreams and visions over more than 40 years, and a continued calling to teach truth and point people toward Jesus Christ.
